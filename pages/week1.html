Introduction to HCI

Human–Computer Interaction (HCI) focuses on designing interactive systems that are usable, accessible, and enjoyable for people.

Key Concepts

  • Usability: Ease of use
  • UX: Overall experience
  • Interaction Design: How users interact
  • Accessibility: Design for everyone

Why HCI Matters

Good HCI improves efficiency, reduces errors, and increases user satisfaction.

John M.Carroll

He says that it no longer makes sense to regard HCI as a specialty of computer science because it has become much more diverse than computer science itself. (The encyclopedia of human-computer interaction 2nd ed.). Now HCI is related to psychology, communication studies, cognitive science, information science and design, in particular with UX/UI design.
